Privacy Policy

Hunter Log is an offline-first gym tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how data is stored, used, and protected when you use the app.

What we collect

Hunter Log does not require account creation or sign-in. Workout logs, body weight entries, progress history, rank data, and personal records are stored locally on your device.

What we do not do

Hunter Log does not sell personal data, does not share your local workout data with third parties, and does not require cloud sync to use core features.

1. Information We Collect

  • Workout session data, including exercises, sets, reps, weights, and progress history.
  • Body weight logs and training-related metrics entered by the user.
  • App preferences stored locally on the device.

Hunter Log does not require a personal account and does not ask users to submit personal identity information for basic use.

2. How Data Is Stored

Core app data is stored locally on the user’s device using local database or storage functionality. The app is designed to work offline, including workout logging, history review, progress tracking, and personal record tracking.

3. Data Collection and Sharing

  • Hunter Log does not collect user workout data to a remote server for core functionality.
  • Hunter Log does not sell, rent, or share user workout data with third parties.
  • If the user manually exports or imports backup data, that action is initiated by the user.

4. Third-Party Services

Hunter Log does not require cloud accounts, social features, or online synchronization for core usage.

If third-party analytics, crash reporting, ads, or similar services are added in a future release, this policy will be updated before those services are used.

5. Data Security

Because data is stored locally, users are responsible for the security of their own devices. We aim to minimize unnecessary data exposure through a local-first app design.

6. Children’s Privacy

Hunter Log is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
